TSA: The Stealing Administration
About this Visualization The Transportation Security Administration is an agency of the U.S. Department of Homeland Security, created in response to 9/11. In the years since 2001 the organization hasn't exactly fostered an outstanding reputation. Privacy and efficiency concerns have been abundant, but the most concerning accusations are those of theft. Nearly 400 TSA agents have been fired for alleged theft over the years and the reports of lost posessions have not stopped. The TSA publishes every passenger claim on their public website. This page looks at that claims data to determine how much theft there really is.
